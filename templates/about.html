Quizify is an application that evaluates how well one truly knows the music they listen to. By connecting with Spotify, you'll receive a test based on snippets of lyrics from your top music.

Quizify was originally created on November 20th, 2021 by the following team of UCI students:
John Lorenzini
Lawrence Lu
Anthony Cusimano
John Daniel Norombaba
Eric Pedley

The project was created as a result of twelve hours of coding at HACK@UCI's ZotHacks 2021.

At ZotHacks 2021, Quizify wowed judges and participants alike with its clever twists in concept and a stunning visual design. As such, the project received the majority vote among participants and mentors, being chosen as the “Hacker's Choice” Winner of the event.

Moving forward, this version of Quizify is updated and maintained by John Lorenzini.

As of the current version, Quizify does not store any data collected. This includes Spotify data such as top music lists or authentication, as well as any lyrics data from Musixmatch.
